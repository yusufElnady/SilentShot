Silent Shot is a first person shooter made by :

  • Ragai Ahmed
  • Yusuf El-Nady
  • Ziyad Eslam
  • Omar Ahmed

It's currently still in development with two maps; a forest and a city. There are also two game modes , which are assassin mode and survival mode, and there is a multiplayer update coming soon!

Assassin Mode

In assasin mode, you will be asked to assassinate a specific person who will be followed by bodyguards, so try to kill him instantly before the bodyguards get you!

Survival Mode

In survival mode, you have to survive as long as possible and kill as many enemies as you can to gain Xp and money to buy new weapons. The enemies will spawn in random positions and will follow you every where you go. You can run but you can't hide !

Compatibility

This game is as compatibile as it is awesome! It is avaliable on Mac Os, windows(from XP and up), Linux, and will soon be avaliable on android and ios. Althogh it's avaliable on nearly all platforms, it might require decent PC/Laptop specifications to function well and play without lagging.
